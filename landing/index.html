The Idea
01

Scout any location on Earth.

Pull real Street View imagery, enhance it with Nano Banana AI, and convert it into a photorealistic 3D Gaussian splat — from your laptop, before you book travel.

02

Block in spatial 3D.

Place cameras, lights, cast marks, crew positions, and props in true three-dimensional space. Assign shot numbers and plan your sequence the way you actually think — spatially.

03

Step into the scene in WebXR. Walk the blocking. Orient your crew. Export your shot list as a PDF for the AD. Arrive on location knowing exactly what you're making.

Features
